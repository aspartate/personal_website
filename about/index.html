I am a student, a tinkerer, and a creator.

June 27, 2017 · One minute read

I’m heavily involved in the nonprofit Global Alliance for Medical Innovation, an incubator for budding healthtech startups. I believe that technological solutions are urgently needed to augment and expand the reach of limited healthcare resources in underserved areas around the world. Talk to me anytime about global health, technology, neuroscience, or magic tricks!
